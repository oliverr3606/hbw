Job Specifications

Web Developer

In general, the ideal web developer should have experience in programming languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. They should also be proficient in using web development tools such as Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch, and Figma. Additionally, they should have experience in developing responsive websites and be up-to-date with the latest web development trends and technologies. Excellent problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work well in a team are also essential.

Web Developer

The ideal web designer should have a strong portfolio of creative and visually appealing designs for websites and digital products. They should be proficient in design tools such as Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch, and Figma. Knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript is also essential for the role. Additionally, they should have experience in designing responsive websites and be up-to-date with the latest web design trends and technologies. Excellent communication skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work well in a team are also essential.

Project Manager

The ideal project manager should have experience in managing web development projects from start to finish. They should be proficient in project management tools such as Asana, Trello, or Jira. Additionally, they should have experience in working with cross-functional teams, including designers, developers, and stakeholders. Excellent communication skills, attention to detail, and the ability to manage timelines and budgets are also essential. Experience in Agile methodology, risk management, and conflict resolution is a plus. Finally, they should be able to motivate and lead their team to deliver high-quality projects on time and within budget.
